About Me

Hello, and welcome to my profile! I am a dedicated husband and father with a passion for adventure. My personal interests include long-distance trail running, electronic dance music (EDM), motorcycles, and reading. I have led an exciting and interesting life thus far. I was born and raised in rural Pennsylvania where my family instilled a love of the outdoors in me. I was a member of the Boy Scouts of America where I learned many valuable lessons. One of the most important lessons I learned growing up is the value of hard work and persistence. I began college in the traditional way immediately after highschool but left prior to completing a degree following the birth of my first child. I then relocated to Hawaii where I worked as a police officer for over 10 years. During this career I served as a patrol officer, motorcycle traffic officer and as a drug-impaired driving enforcement expert and instructor. Once my family life allowed it, I returned to college and resumed my pursuit of knowledge while continuing to work as a police officer. I completed my Associates of Science in Natural Science degree at Leeward Community College where I was recognized two consecutive years by the American Chemical Society as an outstanding student in Chemistry.

At this point, I reached a crossroads in my life where I faced a decision of continuing my career in law enforcement or pursuing higher education with an intent to change careers. I chose to resign from my position as a police officer and relocate to Texas to continue my education. I immediately enrolled in The Coding Boot Camp at the University of Texas and began my studies 2 days after arriving in Austin. During this course, I learned this skills of full-stack web development. I intend to continue my education at the University of Texas with a final goal of earning a PhD in Chemistry.

Thoughout my life, I have amassed a broad skill set including: critical thinking and observation, conflict resolution, public speaking, effective communication, and leadership. I have extensive knowledge of mathematics and natural sciences. I have excelled as a lifelong learner with a demonstrated passion for the pursuit of self-improvement. I am able to function effectively in the roles of leader, teacher, student, or teammate as situation dictates.
